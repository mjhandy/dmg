SEO (Search Engine Optimization) improves website visibility by enhancing content, structure, and performance to rank higher in search engine results, driving organic traffic and engagement.

WCAG (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines) are standards ensuring digital content is perceivable, operable, understandable, and robust—making websites accessible to people with disabilities.

UX (User Experience) encompasses a user's emotions, perceptions, and interactions with a product or service, aiming for intuitive, enjoyable, and accessible design and functionality.
