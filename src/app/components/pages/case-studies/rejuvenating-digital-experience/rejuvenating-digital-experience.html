Background

We’re a small, family-run wellness spa based in coastal Nova Scotia. For over a decade, we’ve offered massage therapy, facials, and holistic treatments in a tranquil setting. Our loyal clientele—locals and seasonal visitors alike—value our personal touch, calming atmosphere, and commitment to natural healing.

But while our in-person experience was thriving, our digital presence told a different story.

Our website, built years ago by a friend of the family, had become outdated. It was slow to load, hard to navigate on mobile, and didn’t reflect the warmth or professionalism of our spa. Clients often called to book appointments because they couldn’t figure out our online form. Worse, we were losing potential new clients who never made it past the homepage.

We knew we needed help—but we didn’t want to lose the heart of who we are. That’s when we found Dundee Media Group.

The Challenge

From our first conversation, Dundee understood that this wasn’t just about a website. It was about preserving our identity while making it easier for people to connect with us.

Together, we identified three key challenges:

  1. Outdated Design: The site felt cluttered and didn’t reflect our spa’s serene, minimalist aesthetic.
  2. Broken User Flow: Clients struggled to find service information, pricing, or the booking form. Many gave up before completing a booking.
  3. Accessibility Gaps: Colour contrast issues, missing alt text, and poor mobile responsiveness made the site difficult to use for many visitors.

We needed a partner who could bring clarity, empathy, and technical know-how. Dundee delivered on all fronts.

The Approach

Dundee began with a discovery phase that felt more like a conversation than a consultation. They asked thoughtful questions—not just about our services, but about our values, our clients, and what makes our spa special.

They then conducted a full audit of our existing site, combining usability testing with accessibility evaluation. We were surprised (and a little embarrassed) by how many friction points they uncovered: confusing navigation, inconsistent page layouts, and a booking form that didn’t work on mobile.

Dundee’s redesign focused on three pillars: simplicity, serenity, and accessibility.

But instead of overwhelming us with jargon or criticism, Dundee translated their findings into a clear, compassionate roadmap. Their approach was collaborative, not prescriptive. We felt like we were building something together.

The Solution

Dundee’s redesign focused on three pillars: simplicity, serenity, and accessibility.

Visual Refresh

  • New Colour Palette: Inspired by our spa’s natural materials—driftwood, sage, and sea stone—the new palette evokes calm and clarity.
  • Typography & Layout: Clean, legible fonts and generous white space created a sense of ease and openness.
  • Photography Direction: Dundee helped us commission new lifestyle photography that captured the warmth of our team and the peacefulness of our space.

User Flow Overhaul

  • Streamlined Navigation: Services, pricing, and booking are now accessible within one or two clicks from any page.
  • Service Pages: Each treatment now has its own page with clear descriptions, durations, and pricing—plus FAQs to reduce phone inquiries.
  • Booking Integration: Dundee rebuilt our booking form with mobile-first design, inline validation, and calendar sync. Clients can now book in under 90 seconds.

Accessibility & Performance

  • WCAG Compliance: Colour contrast, alt text, ARIA labels, and keyboard navigation were all brought up to WCAG 2.1 AA standards.
  • Mobile Optimization: The site now loads 3x faster on mobile and adapts beautifully to all screen sizes.
  • Analytics Setup: Custom events track form completions, bounce rates, and engagement from users with assistive tech—giving us real data to guide future improvements.

The Results

The impact was immediate and measurable:

  • Online bookings increased by 68% in the first three months post-launch.
  • Phone inquiries dropped by 40%, freeing up our front desk team.
  • Mobile bounce rate decreased by 52%, thanks to faster load times and clearer navigation.
  • Client feedback has been glowing, with many commenting on how “peaceful” and “easy to use” the new site feels.

But beyond the numbers, the biggest win was how the new site made us feel. For the first time, our digital presence truly reflects who we are: calm, welcoming, and deeply human.
