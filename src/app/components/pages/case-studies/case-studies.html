Driving Digital Success Through SEO, Accessibility, and Usability

At Dundee Media Group, we believe that every digital experience should be discoverable, inclusive, and effortless to use. Our case studies highlight how we’ve partnered with clients to overcome challenges in search engine optimisation (SEO), accessibility compliance (WCAG), and usability—while also implementing robust Google Analytics tracking to measure impact and guide future growth.

Elevating Visibility with SEO

Many of our clients came to us with websites that weren’t reaching their full potential in search rankings. Through a combination of technical audits, keyword strategy, and content optimization, we helped them climb the search results and attract the right audiences.

  • Technical SEO fixes: We addressed issues like broken links, slow load times, and poor mobile responsiveness.
  • Content strategy: By refining messaging and aligning copy with user intent, we boosted organic traffic and improved engagement.
  • Structured data: Implementing schema markup ensured that our clients’ content was more visible and better understood by search engines.

The result? Increased visibility, higher-quality leads, and measurable growth in organic traffic.

Building Inclusive Experiences with WCAG Compliance

Accessibility isn’t just a requirement—it’s a responsibility. Several clients faced challenges meeting Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG), which limited their reach and excluded potential users.

We worked closely with design and development teams to:

  • Ensure proper colour contrast and scalable typography.
  • Add alt text and ARIA labels for screen reader compatibility.
  • Simplify navigation structures for users with diverse needs.

By embedding accessibility into the design process, we helped clients create digital experiences that are welcoming to all users. This not only improved compliance but also strengthened brand reputation and trust.

Enhancing Usability for Seamless Journeys

A website’s usability can make or break the customer experience. We identified friction points—confusing navigation, cluttered layouts, and unclear calls-to-action—and transformed them into intuitive, user-friendly pathways.

  • User testing: Real feedback guided design refinements.
  • Streamlined flows: We reduced unnecessary steps in forms and checkout processes.
  • Responsive design: Ensuring consistency across devices improved satisfaction and retention.

Clients saw measurable improvements in engagement metrics, with users spending more time on-site and converting at higher rates.

Measuring Success with Google Analytics

Optimisation is only meaningful if it can be measured. That’s why we implemented Google Analytics across client projects, tailoring dashboards to track KPIs that mattered most.

  • Custom event tracking: From button clicks to video plays, we captured the interactions that drive business goals.
  • Conversion funnels: Clear visibility into drop-off points allowed us to refine user journeys.
  • Data-driven insights: Regular reporting empowered clients to make informed decisions and continuously improve.

Analytics became the backbone of our strategy—turning insights into action and ensuring every improvement was backed by evidence.

The Impact

Across industries, our clients experienced tangible results: stronger search rankings, accessible and inclusive platforms, smoother user experiences, and data-driven growth strategies. Each case study demonstrates how thoughtful design, technical expertise, and strategic measurement can transform digital challenges into opportunities.
