Background

A mid-sized professional services firm approached Dundee Media Group with a pressing issue: their website looked polished but underperformed. Users struggled to find information, forms were difficult to complete, and the site fell short of Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG). These gaps limited reach, increased abandonment, and undermined trust in the brand.

The Challenge

  • Usability barriers: Inconsistent information architecture, buried calls-to-action, and weak mobile responsiveness led to task abandonment and low conversions.
  • Accessibility gaps: Colour contrast failed WCAG thresholds, images lacked alt text, and interactive elements were missing labels for assistive technologies.

The client needed a holistic solution that improved user experience while ensuring inclusivity and compliance without sacrificing visual quality.

Insights guided a focused roadmap spanning design, content, and development.

Our approach

We began with a comprehensive audit, pairing usability testing with accessibility evaluation to pinpoint friction and compliance issues in tandem. Insights guided a focused roadmap spanning design, content, and development.

Usability enhancements

  • Simplified navigation: Re-structured menus and clearer pathways to key services.
  • Streamlined forms: Fewer fields, clearer labels and instructions, and accessible inline error messaging.
  • Responsive design: Layouts refined for consistent experiences across desktop, tablet, and mobile.

Accessibility improvements

  • Colour and contrast: Adjusted palettes to meet WCAG 2.1 AA contrast ratios for text and UI components.
  • Alt text and ARIA: Descriptive alt attributes and ARIA labelling for interactive controls and landmarks.
  • Keyboard navigation: Full keyboard operability, visible focus states, and logical tab order.

We collaborated closely with the client’s team, delivering training and documentation so accessibility and usability remain core to future updates.

Measurement with Google Analytics

To validate impact, we implemented Google Analytics with custom events and goals. Dashboards tracked form completions, bounce rates on key landing pages, and engagement patterns for users relying on assistive technologies. Data informed ongoing optimisation and prioritisation.

Results

  • Usability gains: Form completion rates rose by 42%, and average session duration increased.
  • Accessibility compliance: Achieved WCAG 2.1 AA alignment across templates and core interactions.
  • Brand trust: Positive feedback cited clarity, inclusivity, and ease of use as standout improvements.

Conclusion

By solving usability and WCAG challenges in tandem, Dundee Media Group transformed the site into an inclusive, high-performing platform. With analytics grounding each decision, the client gained confidence that improvements were not only experience-led but also evidenced by measurable results.
